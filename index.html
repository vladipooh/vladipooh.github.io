About Me

Data Scientist & Doctoral Researcher

Phuwadol Viroonluecha is a Ph.D. student and early-stage researcher in GIRTEL, Department of Information and Communications Technology at Technical University of Cartagena, Spain. Currently, he researches the application of machine learning for blood glucose control in type I diabetic patients.

In the past, he has worked with leading international companies such as Microsoft and Refinitiv, bringing his data science skills to apply in a variety of fields such as fintech infrastructure, marketing, recruiting, career guidance, and health care.

He is ambitious with high effort, strategic, supportive, a great team work player. He enjoys making plans based on thorough data analysis. He is skilled at presentation and visualization. He enjoys learning new things.
